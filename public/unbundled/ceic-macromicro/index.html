CEIC / Haver Analytics / MacroMicro: what you're actually paying for

Yes — rebuildable from free public data

CEIC / Haver Analytics / MacroMicro typically costs MacroMicro $27–600/mo; CEIC/Haver institutional five-figures. Its core dataset is not proprietary: it is built on FRED (800k+ series); BLS, BEA, Eurostat, ECB, national statistics offices — public sources you can access directly for free. What the subscription actually adds is aggregation and charting of statistics that national agencies publish free: FRED, BLS, Eurostat, national central banks.
