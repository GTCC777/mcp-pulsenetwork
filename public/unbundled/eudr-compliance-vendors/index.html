EUDR deforestation-compliance platforms (various): what you're actually paying for

Yes — rebuildable from free public data

EUDR deforestation-compliance platforms (various) typically costs $15,000–$500,000/yr. Its core dataset is not proprietary: it is built on Copernicus Sentinel imagery; Global Forest Watch / Hansen forest-change data — public sources you can access directly for free. What the subscription actually adds is workflow over free satellite data: the deforestation determination itself rides on open imagery.

The price math

Per-plot checks at $0.50–2.00 vs $15k+ platform minimums — the deadline-driven long tail (small importers) is unserved.
