Healthcare exclusion / sanction screening services: what you're actually paying for

Yes — rebuildable from free public data

Healthcare exclusion / sanction screening services typically costs $1,000s–$10,000s/yr per organization. Its core dataset is not proprietary: it is built on NPPES NPI registry; OIG LEIE exclusion list; SAM.gov exclusions — public sources you can access directly for free. What the subscription actually adds is batch UX over three free federal lists every provider can query directly.
