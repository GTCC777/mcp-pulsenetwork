Morningstar Direct — fund holdings sleeve: what you're actually paying for

Partially — some of it is free public data, some is genuinely proprietary

Morningstar Direct — fund holdings sleeve typically costs $17,500/yr per seat. Part of what it sells is free public data (SEC N-PORT filings (complete portfolio holdings)); part is genuinely proprietary. Fund holdings themselves are free: every registered fund files N-PORT with the SEC monthly (public quarterly). Morningstar's proprietary value is its category taxonomy, star ratings, and analyst layer — that part is NOT arbitragable.
