Commercial recall-monitoring services: what you're actually paying for

Yes — rebuildable from free public data

Commercial recall-monitoring services typically costs $10,000–$50,000/yr. Its core dataset is not proprietary: it is built on CPSC recalls API; openFDA food/drug/device enforcement; EU RASFF / Safety Gate — public sources you can access directly for free. What the subscription actually adds is aggregation of recall feeds that safety agencies publish free, globally.
