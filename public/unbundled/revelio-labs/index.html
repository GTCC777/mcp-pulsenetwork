Revelio Labs workforce intelligence: what you're actually paying for

Partially — some of it is free public data, some is genuinely proprietary

Revelio Labs workforce intelligence typically costs ~$85,000 (AWS Data Exchange listed price). Part of what it sells is free public data (BLS OEWS (occupational wages); O*NET (skills/task taxonomy); DOL LCA + PERM disclosure files (visa wages, employers)); part is genuinely proprietary. Two layers: (1) analytics over free government labor data — arbitragable; (2) a scraped professional-profile panel — NOT safely rebuildable (see traps).

The free public data underneath

SourceLicenseAccess

Rebuild recipe

  1. Wage benchmarking from OEWS; skills mapping from O*NET
  2. H-1B/PERM employer wage red-flag checks from DOL disclosure files
  3. Do NOT scrape job postings or profiles

DIY difficulty: low for the government layer

The price math

Government-layer wage/visa analytics per-call at $0.10–0.50 vs $85k bulk.

Licensing and legal traps

  • hiQ v. LinkedIn ended in a $500k judgment and injunction — profile/posting scraping is not a safe foundation
  • O*NET requires attribution
