How it works

1. Register a key

Self-serve, instant. You get a pk_live_… key with a $0.25 free trial balance — enough to test real calls before you ever pay us anything.

2. Call any endpoint

Send it as x-internal-key on any PulseNetwork request. Each call deducts wholesale cost (50% of that endpoint's retail price, floored at $0.005) from your balance — no per-call crypto payment, no wallet in your hot path.

3. Top up when low

Deposits are themselves an x402 call: hit the deposit URL, pay the 402 in USDC (Base or Solana), and your balance credits automatically the moment it settles. On-chain-verified — no invoicing, no trust required.

When your balance hits zero, wholesale pricing simply stops — calls fall through to the normal retail x402 gate (a real payment would then be required through whatever path you've built, or the call fails). There's no overdraft and no surprise bill.
